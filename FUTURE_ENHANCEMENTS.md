# Future Enhancements

## 1. Online Book Reservation

Students can reserve books online before visiting the library.

## 2. Email Notifications

Automatic email alerts for:
- Due dates
- Late returns
- New book arrivals

## 3. QR Code Integration

Books can be issued and returned by scanning QR codes.

## 4. Mobile Application

Develop Android and iOS applications for easier access.

## 5. Advanced Search

Search books by:
- Title
- Author
- Category
- Publication Year

## 6. Fine Management

Automatically calculate late return penalties.

## 7. Digital Library

Allow students to access e-books and digital resources.

## 8. Analytics Dashboard

Generate reports on:
- Most borrowed books
- Active students
- Library usage statistics
