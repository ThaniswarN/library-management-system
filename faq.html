Frequently Asked Questions

How many books can a student borrow?

A student can borrow up to 3 books at a time.

How long can I keep a book?

Books can be kept for 14 days before renewal or return.

Can I renew a borrowed book?

Yes, if no other student has reserved the book.

What happens if I return a book late?

A late fee may be applied according to library rules.
